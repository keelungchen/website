Research
研究計畫

My research explores the ecology, resilience, and conservation of coral reef ecosystems across the Indo-Pacific. Below is a chronological overview of my projects, from the most recent to earlier work.

我的研究探討珊瑚礁生態系統的生態學、韌性與保育議題，研究範圍涵蓋整個印度洋—太平洋地區。以下依時間順序呈現各研究計畫，由最新至較早期的工作。

Natural Reef Crevice Geometry as a Driver of Juvenile Invertebrate Recruitment
自然礁體縫隙幾何特徵對無脊椎動物幼生補充的影響
Applying SfM photogrammetry and GIS analysis to quantify crevice geometry on natural reefs — width, depth, rugosity, fractal dimension — and linking these structural metrics to juvenile invertebrate density and post-settlement survival. Bridging the gap between artificial-substrate experiments and natural reef recruitment dynamics. 利用 SfM 攝影測量與 GIS 分析量化自然礁體縫隙幾何特徵（寬度、深度、粗糙度、碎形維度），並連結至幼生密度與定著後存活率。填補人工基質實驗與自然礁體幼生補充動態之間的知識缺口。

Morphological and Spatial Determinants of Coral Demography
珊瑚群體形態與空間因子對族群動態的影響
Integrating colony morphology and 3D spatial positioning into demographic models using SfM photogrammetry and AI segmentation, tracking thousands of coral colonies across Taiwan, Hawai'i, and Australia to reveal how shape and spatial competition drive population dynamics. 結合 SfM 攝影測量與 AI 分割技術，將群體形態與三維空間定位整合進族群模型，追蹤橫跨台灣、夏威夷與澳洲的數千個珊瑚群體，揭示形態與空間競爭如何驅動族群動態。

Taiwan-wide Coral Reef 3D Survey (SfM Photogrammetry)
全台珊瑚礁 3D（SfM 攝影測量）調查
Developing SfM photogrammetry SOPs and GIS-based coral population dynamics analysis for a nationwide 3D reef monitoring network — in collaboration with Fan Coral Lab (NMMBA), covering Kenting, Xiaoliuqiu, Green Island, Orchid Island, and beyond. 與海生館 Fan Coral Lab 合作，開發 SfM 攝影測量標準作業流程與基於 GIS 的珊瑚族群動態分析，建立涵蓋墾丁、小琉球、綠島、蘭嶼等地的全台 3D 珊瑚礁監測網絡。

Octocorals of Taiwan — A Comprehensive Field Guide
台灣八放珊瑚圖鑑
Collaborated with Prof. Chang-Feng Dai (NTU) to produce the definitive illustrated guide to Taiwan's octocoral fauna, contributing field photography, sclerite microphotography, and bilingual manuscript proofreading. Chinese edition published in 2022 by Owl Publishing House; English edition published in 2021 by National Taiwan University Press. 協助台灣大學戴昌鳳教授編撰台灣八放珊瑚圖鑑，負責野外攝影、骨針顯微攝影及中英文書稿校對。中文版已於 2022 年由貓頭鷹出版社出版；英文版已於 2021 年由國立臺灣大學出版中心出版。

Underwater 3D Survey Outreach Workshops
水下 3D 調查推廣工作坊

Quantitative Analysis of Coral Reef Three-Dimensional Structure
珊瑚礁立體結構量化分析研究 — 以基隆市潮境海灣資源保育區為例
Applying Structure-from-Motion photogrammetry and GIS spatial analysis to quantify coral reef 3D structure at Chaojing Bay, producing millimetre-resolution DEMs for benthic habitat characterisation. 應用運動恢復結構攝影測量與 GIS 空間分析，量化潮境海灣珊瑚礁立體結構，建立毫米級解析度的數值高程模型以進行底棲棲地特徵分析。
