Overview計畫概述

Taiwan's coral reefs span a latitudinal gradient of nearly 10°, making them an ideal natural laboratory for studying how thermal history shapes bleaching tolerance. This project quantifies thermal stress thresholds across dominant reef-building genera — including Acropora, Porites, and Montipora — at sites from subtropical Kenting to the more tropical Orchid Island.

台灣珊瑚礁橫跨近 10° 緯度梯度，是研究熱歷史如何塑造白化耐受性的天然實驗室。本計畫針對主要造礁珊瑚屬（包括鹿角珊瑚、濱珊瑚與葉狀珊瑚）在墾丁至蘭嶼各礁點量化熱壓力閾值。

Research Questions研究問題

  • Do corals at warmer, lower-latitude sites exhibit higher thermal tolerance than those at higher latitudes?
    緯度較低、水溫較高礁區的珊瑚，是否比高緯度礁區的珊瑚具有更高的熱耐受性？
  • Which coral genera are most vulnerable to projected end-of-century warming scenarios?
    在本世紀末升溫情境下，哪些珊瑚屬最為脆弱？
  • Can we identify bleaching-resistant genotypes or symbiont communities that could inform assisted evolution strategies?
    能否鑑別出可供人為演化策略參考的耐白化基因型或共生體群落？

Key Findings So Far目前主要發現

Preliminary analyses from 2023–2024 field seasons reveal significant latitudinal variation in bleaching onset temperatures: corals at Orchid Island consistently bleach at temperatures ~0.8°C higher than their Kenting counterparts, suggesting thermal acclimatisation across the gradient. Symbiodiniaceae community composition varies markedly between sites, with heat-tolerant Clade D symbionts more prevalent at the warmer southern sites.

A key methodological advance for this project came from a collaborative validation study (McRae et al. 2024, Coral Reefs): CBASS-derived ED50 thermal tolerance values were groundtruthed against reef-scale bleaching patterns captured through large-area photogrammetric imaging. The results confirmed that short-term laboratory heat assays reliably predict field bleaching susceptibility, providing confidence that our bench-scale screening can be scaled up to population-level predictions across the Taiwan reef network.

2023–2024 年田野季的初步分析顯示，各礁點的白化起始溫度存在顯著的緯度差異：蘭嶼的珊瑚始終比墾丁珊瑚在約高 0.8°C 時才開始白化，顯示沿緯度梯度存在熱馴化現象。各礁點的 Symbiodiniaceae 群落組成差異顯著，耐熱的 D 族共生藻在較溫暖的南部礁點更為普遍。

本計畫在方法論上的重要突破，來自一項合作驗證研究（McRae et al. 2024，Coral Reefs）：透過大面積攝影測量影像所捕捉的礁體尺度白化模式，對 CBASS 實驗所得的 ED50 熱耐受值進行現地驗證。結果確認短期實驗室熱壓測定可靠地預測現地白化易感性，讓我們確信室內篩選結果可延伸至台灣礁體網絡的族群尺度預測。
