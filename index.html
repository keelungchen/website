ABOUT
關於我

I am a coral reef ecologist based in Taiwan, investigating how climate change, human disturbance, and ecological processes shape reef communities. My work integrates underwater surveys, remote sensing, and ecological modelling to understand reef resilience and inform conservation strategy.

我是一位珊瑚礁生態學家，目前任職於台灣。研究聚焦於氣候變遷、人為干擾與生態過程如何共同塑造礁體群聚結構，結合水下調查、遙測技術與生態模型，探討珊瑚礁的韌性並提供保育決策依據。

Coral Reef Ecology 珊瑚礁生態 Climate Change 氣候變遷 Coral Bleaching 珊瑚白化 Underwater Survey 水下調查 Remote Sensing 遙測 Conservation 保育

I completed my Ph.D. in Marine Biology and have been conducting long-term monitoring of reef communities across the Indo-Pacific. I am passionate about communicating science to the public and have contributed to both academic journals and popular science media.

When I am not diving, I write about ocean science and collaborate with photographers and filmmakers to bring reef stories to wider audiences.

取得海洋生物學博士學位後，我長期在印度洋—太平洋地區進行礁體群聚監測。我同時致力於科學傳播，曾在學術期刊與科普媒體發表作品，也與攝影師及紀錄片工作者合作，將珊瑚礁的故事帶給更廣泛的受眾。
