ABOUT
關於我
放置頭像
photos/avatar.jpg

I am a Ph.D. student at the Hawaiʻi Institute of Marine Biology (HIMB), University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa, working under Dr. Joshua Madin in the Marine Conservation Innovation Group. My research focuses on the structural complexity and morphology of coral reefs to better understand reef health and ecosystem function. I utilise advanced metrics — including fractal dimension and circularity — integrated with deep learning models and spatial analysis tools such as ArcGIS Pro and Agisoft Metashape to quantify the intricate physical patterns of reef environments.

我目前是夏威夷大學馬諾阿分校夏威夷海洋生物研究所（HIMB）的博士生，在 Joshua Madin 博士指導下進行研究。研究聚焦於珊瑚礁的結構複雜度與形態，以深入了解礁體健康與生態系功能。我運用碎形維度、圓形度等進階指標，結合深度學習模型及 ArcGIS Pro、Agisoft Metashape 等空間分析工具，量化礁體環境的複雜物理結構。

3D Photogrammetry 三維攝影測量 Coral Reef Ecology 珊瑚礁生態 Population Dynamics 族群動態 Structural Complexity 結構複雜度 Geospatial Analysis 地理空間分析 Deep Learning 深度學習

I am particularly interested in the population dynamics of corals and their relationship with morphological traits. I lead 3D photogrammetric monitoring projects across reef sites in Taiwan and am committed to open data sharing to advance reef science. My academic background spans a M.Sc. in Oceanography from National Taiwan University and a B.S. in Oceanography from National Sun Yat-Sen University.

Outside research, I maintain a personal interest in octocoral taxonomy and enjoy bringing reef science to broader audiences through photography and fieldwork documentation.

我尤其關注珊瑚的族群動態及其與形態特徵的關係，並主持台灣多個礁點的三維攝影測量監測計畫，致力於開放資料共享以推動礁體科學研究。學術背景方面，我擁有國立台灣大學海洋學碩士學位，以及國立中山大學海洋學學士學位。研究之餘，對八放珊瑚分類學抱有個人興趣，並透過水下攝影記錄珊瑚礁的故事。
