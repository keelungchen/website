Overview計畫概述

Structure-from-Motion (SfM) photogrammetry is transforming how we document and analyse coral reef ecosystems, yet the technique remains inaccessible to many researchers and conservation practitioners in Taiwan. This workshop series aims to bridge that gap — providing hands-on training in underwater 3D survey techniques, 3D model generation, and quantitative analysis, so that more teams across Taiwan can adopt standardised, internationally comparable methods for reef monitoring and research.

By making Taiwan's coral reef survey methodology and data compatible with international standards, we contribute to the growing global effort of reef monitoring using 3D photogrammetry — as practised by institutions such as the Scripps Institution of Oceanography (100 Island Challenge), NOAA, and the University of Hawai'i.

運動恢復結構（SfM）攝影測量正在改變珊瑚礁生態系的記錄與分析方式，但這項技術在台灣對許多研究者與保育實務工作者而言仍有門檻。本工作坊系列旨在填補這個落差——提供水下 3D 調查技術、三維模型建構與定量分析的實作訓練，讓台灣更多團隊能夠採用標準化、與國際接軌的珊瑚礁監測與研究方法。

透過讓台灣珊瑚礁的調查方法與資料與國際標準接軌，我們也為全球日益成長的 3D 攝影測量珊瑚礁監測工作做出貢獻——與 Scripps 海洋學研究所（100 Island Challenge）、NOAA、夏威夷大學等機構的研究方法一致。

Workshop Curriculum工作坊課程內容

The workshop is structured as a two-day intensive programme (3 hours per day), combining lectures with hands-on exercises. Participants bring their own laptops, cameras, and even a personal object to practise 3D scanning. All course materials — slides, scripts, sample data — are open-sourced on GitHub.

工作坊為期兩天密集課程（每天 3 小時），結合講座與實作練習。學員自備筆電、相機，甚至可攜帶個人物品進行 3D 掃描練習。所有課程教材——簡報、程式碼、範例資料——皆開源於 GitHub。

Day 1第一天

  • Introduction to 3D survey & SfM principles
    3D 調查方法與 SfM 原理介紹
  • Colony-scale photography practice
    群體尺度攝影實作
  • Habitat-scale photography practice
    棲地尺度攝影實作
  • Metashape software operation (90 min)
    Metashape 軟體操作（90 分鐘）
  • 3D model export & Sketchfab visualisation
    3D 模型輸出與 Sketchfab 視覺化

Day 2第二天

  • Georeferencing, DEM & Orthomosaic generation
    地理參照、DEM 與正射影像生成
  • Real-world case studies & analysis techniques
    實際案例與分析技術
  • Colony-scale model refinement in Metashape
    群體尺度模型精修
  • R-based 3D complexity analysis (habtools)
    R 語言 3D 複雜度分析（habtools）
  • QGIS + R for habitat-scale spatial analysis

Impact & Goals影響與目標

The workshop series has been delivered to researchers and students from the National Museum of Marine Biology and Aquarium (Fan Coral Lab), the Delta Electronics Foundation's coral monitoring team, and National Sun Yat-sen University's Macrae Marine Ecology Lab. By training multiple research groups in the same standardised workflow, we ensure data interoperability and build a growing community of practice around 3D reef survey in Taiwan.

Our broader goals are threefold: first, to lower the technical barrier so that more Taiwanese marine researchers can adopt SfM photogrammetry in their work; second, to align Taiwan's reef survey data with international standards (WGS 84 georeferencing, Agisoft Metashape workflows, open data formats) so that datasets can be directly compared with global coral monitoring programmes; and third, to promote open science by releasing all teaching materials, code, and sample data publicly on GitHub.

本工作坊系列已為國立海洋生物博物館（Fan Coral Lab）、台達電子文教基金會珊瑚監測團隊、以及國立中山大學 Macrae Marine Ecology Lab 的研究人員與學生提供培訓。透過讓多個研究團隊學習相同的標準化流程，我們確保資料的互通性，並在台灣建立日益壯大的 3D 珊瑚礁調查實踐社群。

我們的更廣泛目標有三：第一，降低技術門檻，讓更多台灣海洋研究者能在工作中採用 SfM 攝影測量；第二，讓台灣珊瑚礁調查資料與國際標準接軌（WGS 84 地理參照、Agisoft Metashape 工作流程、開放資料格式），使資料集能直接與全球珊瑚監測計畫進行比較；第三，推動開放科學，將所有教材、程式碼與範例資料公開發布於 GitHub。

Open Source Materials開源教材

All workshop materials are freely available on GitHub, including presentation slides, Metashape SOP documentation, R analysis scripts (using habtools, rgl, Rvcg, raster, sf, ggplot2), QGIS quick-start guides, and sample datasets. The repository is designed so that anyone — even those new to GitHub — can download the materials and work through the exercises independently.

所有工作坊教材皆免費公開於 GitHub，包括簡報投影片、Metashape SOP 文件、R 分析腳本（使用 habtools、rgl、Rvcg、raster、sf、ggplot2）、QGIS 快速入門指南，以及範例資料集。教材設計讓任何人——即使是 GitHub 新手——都能下載並獨立完成練習。
